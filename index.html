About

Purpose-driven work for people who care.

I'm Misse Baker — a Purdue University graduate with nearly two decades of experience working alongside some of the most mission-driven organizations in the country, including Scouting America, the American Cancer Society, and Reach For Youth.

What I've learned from a career in the nonprofit world is that the people doing the most important work are often the least equipped to tell their story. That's where I come in.

I founded Purpose Made LLC because I believe every organization — and every entrepreneur — deserves a brand that matches the size of their mission. I help nonprofits and small businesses build beautiful websites, grow their social media presence, design creative marketing campaigns, and plan events that leave a lasting impression.

Party planning with a purpose. Strategy with heart. That's what I do.
